```
  Search: "Halo Infinite weapon wireframe breakdown ArtStation"
  Search: "near-future weapon hard surface bevel reference"

BEVEL RULE (written, pinned above monitor before modeling):
  "2 loops at 45°. Every hard edge. No exceptions except energy cell housing."

WHY THIS WIDTH:
  At 512px/m texel density and FPS camera distance, a 1-loop bevel is too tight —
  the specular highlight is nearly invisible in motion.
  A 3-loop bevel reads as rounded/soft — loses the military hard edge feel.
  2 loops: clear specular catch, reads as machined metal, consistent across all edges.
```
